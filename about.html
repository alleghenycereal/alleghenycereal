Who Knew Granola Could Be This Good? About Allegheny Cereal

Since 2017, Allegheny Cereal has been cooking up an inventive and tasty assortment of granola flavors that can't be found on supermarket shelves. Established in Pittsburgh's historic Deutschtown neighborhood, we source high-quality local ingredients whenever possible.

Our granola is made with love in small batches. We are always experimenting and adding new flavors, so check back often to see what's in store.
